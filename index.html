About Our Safari Tours

Mogarkasa is a serene and ecologically rich forest reserve located approximately 75–80 kilometers from Nagpur, Maharashtra, near the town of Paoni. Spanning around 16 square kilometers, it forms part of the Ramdham–Ramtek–Nagardhan–Mogarkasa–Pench tourism circuit, making it an accessible destination for nature enthusiasts and eco-tourists n 2020, the Maharashtra Forest Department initiated an eco-tourism project to revitalize Mogarkasa. The plan includes constructing six eco-huts near the lake to provide sustainable accommodation for visitors. 1. Natural Beauty & Wildlife 2. Trekking & Activities
